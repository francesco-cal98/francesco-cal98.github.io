About Me

I'm an ML Engineer and AI Research Scientist working at the intersection of computer vision, time-series forecasting, and agentic AI, building systems that don't just run in notebooks, but in production.

My work spans deep learning research and applied engineering: from multimodal architectures and Bayesian cognitive models at the University of Padova, to demand forecasting platforms and LLM-powered agents that reason, retrieve, and act autonomously.

I believe the most impactful AI sits at the boundary between rigorous research and pragmatic engineering.

Numerical Representation Analysis Pipeline

Modular toolkit for analyzing how deep networks represent numerical concepts. Seven analysis stages: power-law scaling, linear probes, geometry (RSA), reconstruction quality, dimensionality reduction (PCA/UMAP/t-SNE), CKA layer comparisons, and behavioral numerosity tasks. Unified adapter system for swapping model architectures without rewriting analysis code.
